About

I am Melody (Zhi Xin) Lee, an enthusiastic learner and problem solver from Singapore. I enjoy crafting creations of various mediums, including designing games and writing poetry. I also like working with people as enthusiastic as I am in making amazing products.

I graduated with a Bachelor in Computing from the National University of Singapore, with a Minor in English. Currently, I am doing what I love - programming games at the Entertainment Technology Center at Carnegie Mellon University.
